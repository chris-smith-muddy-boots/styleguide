Popups / Overlay

Popups are not new windows anymore but a window of information pulled to the front of the page, useful for important actions or alerts.

Try to avoid popups where ever possible, they should be used sparingly.
Try and make these popup windows moveable.
Grey out the rest of the screen behind.
The popup is contained in a 10 pixel wide green border .
