Typography

Our primary font family is Open Sans and it is widely used in the website. This is an open type face which is free to use. https://www.google.com/fonts#UsePlace:use/Collection:Open+Sans This sans serif font is available in various weights. For our purposes, however, the most commonly used are Regular and Bold.

Where possible we are using Open Sans on all the Greenlight products.

Arial is also considered a suitable substitution.

Body Text

The body text is a very dark grey, #333333, sized 12px.

Titles

<h2>

These page titles are size 2em (24px).

<h3>

Sidebar section headings or main content sub headings are 1.5em (18px)

Form Labels

Labels are size 1.1666666667em (14px)

A required asterisk is red #cc0000 and large 1.5em (18px)

Form helper text is normal body text size (1em/12px), dark grey #999999

Also see Forms.

Links

These are green #5ba546 with no underline, lighter green #5bb646 and underlined on rollover.

Top Navigation

The top level links are dark grey #999999, size 1.1666666667em (14px), lighter green #5bb646 on rollover, black #333333 and bold when active.

Logos

The font in our Greenlight Logos is Insignia LT Std Roman. With 26pt for the main title and 18pt for the second line (greenlight). Read more about our Logos.
