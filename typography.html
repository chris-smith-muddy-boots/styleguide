Typography

Our primary font family is Open Sans and it is widely used in the website. This is an Open Type face which is free to use. https://www.google.com/fonts/specimen/Open+Sans This sans serif font is available in various weights. For our purposes, however, the most commonly used are Regular and Bold.

Where possible we are using Open Sans Regular on all the Greenlight products.
Arial is also considered a suitable substitution.
