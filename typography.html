Typography

Our primary font family is Open Sans and it is widely used in the website. This is an Open Type face which is free to use. https://typekit.com/fonts/open-sans This sans serif font is available in various weights. For our purposes, however, the most commonly used are Regular and Bold.

However Arial is also considered a suitable substitution.

Logos

The font in our Greenlight Logos is Insignia LT Std Roman. With 26pt for the main title and 18pt for the second line (greenlight). Read more about our Logos here.
