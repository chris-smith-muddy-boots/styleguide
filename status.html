Status Notifications (Flags)

  • Status notifications, typically appearing on a Dashboard home-screen and describing an action and status of that action, can be used to provide an overview of recent activity.
  • Positive actions such as completion or approval, for example, should be represented in Green
    Something in a mid-state such as pending or waiting should be represented in Amber.
  • Negative or warning actions such as an expiry should be represented in Red/
  • Status flags should be vertical colours 12 pixels wide with word alongside in bold and capitals
