Names

Greenlight consists of the following Products:

  • Greenlight Supplier Approval
  • Greenlight Quality Control
  • Greenlight Assessments
  • Greenlight Grower Management

Think about how you refer to the Greenlight products in any external and internal communications. The Greenlight products should always be referred to in their full names or, if necessary, with abbreviations. Some examples being:

  • Greenlight Quality Control
  • Greenlight QC
  • GLQC
  • Greenlight Grower Management
  • Greenlight GM
  • GLGM

Please do not refer to individual products as Greenlight or Muddy Boots or prefix products names with Muddy Boots For example:

  • Greenlight
  • Muddy Boots
  • Muddy Boots QC
  • Muddy Boots Quality Control
