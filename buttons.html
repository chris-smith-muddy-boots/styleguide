Buttons

  • ​Primary Action buttons should be green (#5ba546) with a lighter rollover (#5bb646)
  • Secondary action button should be dark grey (#666) with a lighter rollover (#777).
  • The Primary action should appear first.
  • All buttons have white text centered.
