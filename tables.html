Tables

Tables and notifications (Status) Notifications, typically appearing on a Dashboard home-screen and describing an action and status of that action, can be used to provide an overview of recent activity.

HTML tables: All left justified. Solid Green, headings should be white #FFFFFF

Alternate table rows #EFF5E5 (pale green) alternate table row (even) background

Status flags should be vertical colours 12 pixels wide with word alongside in bold and capitals.
