Tables

Tables are used throughout the Greenlight product suite and colours and styling should be consistent.

  • HTML tables rows (including title) are left justified
  • Header row should be Bold White text on dark green background
  • The rest of the table has alternating white and green coloured backgrounds.#deedda (pale green)
  • There are no grey horizontal and vertical borders. White vertical cell borders should be considered to seperate the content of each review.
